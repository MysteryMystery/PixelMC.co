About Us

Welcome to PixelMC, the ultimate Minecraft server for Pokemon fans! Pixelmon is a modded server that allows you to catch, train, battle and trade over 900 different Pokemon in a custom Minecraft world. You can join gyms, participate in tournaments, collect badges, and even create your own Pokemon team. PixelMC is more than just a server, it’s a community of passionate Pokemon lovers who enjoy playing together and sharing their adventures. Whether you are a casual player or a hardcore fan, you will find something to love in PixelMC. Join us today and experience the thrill of catching ’em all!
